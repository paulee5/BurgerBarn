About Us

Burger Barn is located at 123 Lexington Avenue in East Jackson, located in Downtown Jackson. It is open from 10 a.m. to 6.p.m on Monday through Saturday. Burger Barn is a local business that was opened in 1979. Shelton and Wanda Fly, opened the resteraunt after they had their first child. The original Burger Barn is located in East Jackson and is still there to this day. They have an extremely loyal staff with Regina, Fred, Kay, and Jennifer working with Wanda for over 20 years, while Robert and Kim have been working for six and four years respectively.
